About

Simran has always had a passion for black and white photography. She believes that is adds timelessness to a photograph, making it an individualistic and classic art piece. She specializes in portrait photography because she believes that the eyes provide the viewer with a glimpse of the eternal soul of the one being photographed.

An open-minded thinker imbibed with the love of all people, Simran loves to connect with people from all walks of life, be it a village or town. Her other passions include food, travel and hip-hop music.

She has won multiple awards in the categories of 'Youth Portrait Photography,' and 'Portraits 2017.'

She aspires to use her photographs to evoke passion amongst those who are a part of the 'Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao' movement and also those that believe in a feminist future in India.

She looks forward to using her art to inspire people all around the globe to unite in order to build a more eco-friendly and socially equal community, free of hatred, disease and fear.
